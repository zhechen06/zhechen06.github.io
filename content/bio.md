I am a **Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellow** in the Department of Building Environment and Energy Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Prof. Fu Xiao on smart and scalable data-driven management technologies for building HVAC systems.

My research sits at the intersection of **building energy systems, machine learning, and intelligent control**, with a focus on HVAC fault diagnosis, energy prediction, demand response, chiller plant optimization, and uncertainty-aware energy-saving verification. I received my PhD from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2024, following an MEng from Tongji University and a BEng from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. My work has been recognized with the **PolyU Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellowship**, the **FCE Awards for Outstanding PhD Theses**, multiple energy analytics competition championships, and highly cited paper awards.
